Getting Started

  1. Open the app → click Connect WhatsApp to complete Meta Embedded Signup.
  2. (Optional) In the API, set API_KEY, WA_TOKEN, WA_PHONE_ID as Render environment variables.
  3. Use Quick Send to test your first message.
  4. Import a CSV (name, phone) and send a small batch.

Do & Don’t

Do
  • Use approved templates for marketing.
  • Get consent and provide opt-out (e.g., “Reply STOP”).
  • Keep numbers in E.164 format (+91…)
Don’t
  • No spam or prohibited goods.
  • Don’t send sensitive PII or health data.
  • Don’t exceed Meta rate limits.
