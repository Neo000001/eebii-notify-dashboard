Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Eebii Notify is a messaging utility that connects your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) to send notifications. We do not sell your data.

What we store

  • Account metadata needed for login/session (e.g., Google user ID) if you opt in to save it.
  • Configuration pointers required to call Meta APIs.
  • Operational logs (errors, delivery statuses) for troubleshooting.

What we do not store

  • Message bodies or attachments by default.
  • Customer phonebooks, unless you explicitly import and save them later (future DB mode).

Third-party services

We use Google (OAuth), Meta (WhatsApp), Render/Cloud hosting. Their privacy policies apply.
